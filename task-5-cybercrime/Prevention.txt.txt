🔒 Stay Safe Online (For College Students in India)

Always verify payments in your bank/UPI app—never trust screenshots.
Do not share OTP, PIN, or UPI passwords with anyone.
Avoid clicking on unknown links in SMS, emails, or WhatsApp.
Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA).
Never accept unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps. (Financial safety)
Double-check UPI IDs before sending money. (Financial safety)
Do not share personal details like Aadhaar, PAN, or bank info casually.
Keep your apps and phone software updated to avoid security bugs.
Use only official apps from Play Store/App Store.
If you face fraud, immediately report it on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
