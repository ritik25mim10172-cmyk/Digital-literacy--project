## Task 5 Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

While researching cybercrime, I was surprised to learn how easily people can be tricked into UPI fraud through simple tactics like fake calls or payment requests. Many victims lose money because they are unaware that receiving money does not require approval.

This made me realize the importance of being cautious while using digital platforms. One habit I will change is that I will never approve unknown payment requests or click on suspicious links. I will also verify all transactions carefully before taking any action.
